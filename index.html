Autonomous 6-DOF Vision-Guided Robotic Arm

A fully autonomous 6-degree-of-freedom robotic arm with an end-to-end perception-to-actuation pipeline. A depth camera feeds 3D perception into a ROS2 stack — containerized with Docker on a Raspberry Pi — that drives serial-bus servos for precise, coordinated multi-joint motion.
